3.1.1.1.1. MPRAGE¶

The three-dimensional magnetization-prepared rapid gradient-echo (3D MPRAGE) [Mugler and Brookeman, 1990] is a commonly used T1w sMRI scanning sequence which optimizes the contrast between different types of tissue and provides anatomical information with outstanding detail. It is a standard sequence, also included in other contemporary large-scale neuroimaging datasets, such as the HCP [Harms et al., 2018], ABIDE II [Di Martino et al., 2017], and ADNI [Hua et al., 2008]. Unfortunately, some variation does still exist in the scanning parameters. The following table summarizes the prevalence of each distinct protocol specification in the included dataset:
